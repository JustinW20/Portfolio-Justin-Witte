About me

Hello, my name is Justin Witte. I am 19 years old and live in Volendam, the Netherlands. I am in the second year of my study ICT Game development at the Hogeschool van Amsterdam. I work parttime at the local grocery store.
I enjoy making games and I love to see people enjoy games that I made, it is amazing to see that I can make people happy by doing something I love doing. I prefer to work in a team rather than alone, as I believe I can learn from others by them simply showing their work and viceversa.
I got into making games by playing them myself, which I have been doing from a really young age starting with games like Pokemon Diamond and New Super Mario bros on the Nintendo DS.
Apart from making games, I like to play many sports, mostly football and to simply run a few kilometres. I also like to play games with friends.
